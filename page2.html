About the Animals

This provides a few pieces of information about the animals in the table. It is split up intp 4 categorys and it tells you what the animal is, what environment they are usually in, if they are a Mammal and the type of diet they are on. Some animals only eat meat which are considered carnivores while others eat only plants which are herbivores. It's very interesting to where you can find these wildlife animals because they are not just regular everyday animal. The zoo or like a resort/safari would be the best place to come across these certain animals and you can just view them from a distance with no harm. Majority of them are friendly and will cause no harm. Each animal has a different lifespan compared to everyone else and it is also interesting to see how different species of snakes last way longer compared to others. As provided in the chart a snake can live up to a minimum of 2 years to a maximum of 30 years depending on what type of snake it is.
